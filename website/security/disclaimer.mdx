## You are running an autonomous program

Lighter MCP exposes Lighter trading to AI agents. AI agents are
**probabilistic**:

- They sometimes misinterpret instructions.
- They sometimes hallucinate context.
- They sometimes chain unrelated actions when they shouldn't.

The safety gates in this server (mode allowlist, per-order cap, daily
cap, leverage cap, two-step confirmation, allowlisted symbols) reduce
blast radius — they do not eliminate it. **Treat this server like a
partially-trusted automation, not a deterministic CLI.**

## What this means in practice

  <Card title="Set the smallest viable caps" icon="gauge">
